Privacy Policy

Introduction

At BizStock, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform. Please read this carefully to understand our practices.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services and improve your experience:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and account details.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including login times, pages visited, and features used.
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed securely via trusted gateways.
  • Communication: Messages exchanged with customer support and notifications preferences.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Provide and maintain our services efficiently.
  • Process transactions and deliver products safely.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, promotions, and support.
  • Personalize your experience and offer relevant product recommendations.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • Improve our platform based on usage analytics and feedback.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these cases:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the platform (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of BizStock, our users, or others.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we continuously work to enhance our defenses.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Raise concerns about privacy or data handling.

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team at support@bizstock.com.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
